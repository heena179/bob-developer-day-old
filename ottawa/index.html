FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Will food be provided?

Yes — breakfast and lunch are complimentary for all attendees. The Code Café runs coffee and refreshments throughout the full day.

Is parking available?

Free onsite parking at IBM Ottawa, 3755 Riverside Dr. No reservation needed.

Do I need to bring my laptop?

Recommended if you want to participate in the hands-on breakout sessions. We'll help you set up Bob during the sessions.

What's the dress code?

Business casual or smart casual. Comfort matters — you'll be building and collaborating all day.

Can I attend just part of the day?

We encourage full-day attendance, but you're welcome to join for the sessions that matter most to you.

Can I bring a colleague?

Absolutely. Please have them register separately so we can plan for the right number of attendees.
